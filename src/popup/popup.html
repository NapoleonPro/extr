System Audio Mode

Captures audio from the current tab or window, not from your microphone.

How to Use

  1. Open the video or meeting you want to transcribe
  2. Click "Start Listening" below
  3. Select the tab or window with audio
  4. Enable "Share audio" in the permission dialog
  5. Click "Share" to begin transcription
